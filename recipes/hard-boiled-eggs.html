Hard Boiled Eggs Recipe

Description

These hard boiled eggs are great sources of protein and choline! Cook up a dozen each week during your food prep and your brain will thank you!

Ingredients

  • A dozen eggs
  • A stock pot
  • Water
  • A stove
  • Towel
  • Ice water bath

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil using your stock pot on the stove.
  2. Once the water comes to a boil; turn off the heat and put the eggs carefully into the water.
  3. Cover the pot and wait 10 minutes.
  4. After 10 minutes, take the eggs out of the stock pot and put them immediately into an ice/water bath.
  5. Let the eggs cool in the bath to stop the cooking process until the eggs are cool to the touch.
  6. Dry off the eggs one by one when taking them out of the ice bath.
  7. Put the eggs back into the carton for storage
