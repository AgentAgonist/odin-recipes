Spaghetti

Description

This is the one meal I remember my mom preparing for the family. This was usually a weekly staple for the family. Nothing fancy. Just holds a special place in me and wanted to share it with you.

Ingredients

  • Noodles of your choice
  • Pinch of salt
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Can of tomatoes
  • Can of mushrooms
  • Pinch of Garlic powder
  • (opt.) Ground beef

Steps

  • Heat your water up to a boil.
  • Put the pinch of salt and a good swig of olive oil in the water.
  • While the water is boiling, put the spaghetti sauce, can of tomatoes, can of mushrooms, and pinch of garlic powder into a pot.
  • Set the pot on medium heat.
  • Once the water starts boiling; put in your noodles.
  • When the spaghetti sauce starts to bubble; you know it is done. Give it a few stirs and set it down to low or warm heat.
  • When your noodles are soft and chewable in the mouth; you know your spaghetti is done.
  • Put some noodles on a plate and add some sauce.
  • Enjoy!
