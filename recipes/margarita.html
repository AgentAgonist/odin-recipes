Margarita Recipe

Description

The margarita is a marvelous way to quench your summer thirst at your next party or get together! You and your guests will love (and be drunk off their tuckus) with this party staple!

Ingredients

  • .75 oz. lime juice
  • .75 oz. agave syrup
  • .75 oz. Cointreau (or triple sec on the cheap)
  • 2 oz. silver tequila
  • Some clean ice
  • 1-2 limes

Steps

  1. Cut a lime and squeeze it.
  2. Put the lime juice, agave syrup, Cointreau, and silver tequila all in the smaller part of your shaker
  3. Put that ice in the other half of your shaker.
  4. Shake it for about 11 or so seconds.
  5. Open the shaker and strain into a cup.
  6. Garnish with a lime wheel and enjoy!
